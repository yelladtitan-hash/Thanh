About

The Artist

Thanh Phong Trinh is a visual artist and painter whose work investigates the tension between graphic precision and painterly intuition. Working primarily in oils and acrylics, his canvases distill reality into its most essential forms.

Influenced by graphic realism and the meditative traditions of East Asian aesthetics, Trinh constructs compositions where negative space becomes as meaningful as painted form — where restraint is itself an act of expression.

Based in Germany, he has exhibited internationally and continues to explore the visual language of quietude.
